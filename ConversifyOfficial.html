Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Conversify and our mental health support services.

Conversify is a comprehensive mental health support platform that combines AI technology with evidence-based resources to provide immediate, accessible support for various mental health challenges. Our platform offers an AI chatbot for immediate conversations, a rich media library of therapeutic resources, ASMR therapy tools, and connections to professional help when needed. We aim to make mental health support available to everyone, everywhere, at any time.

No, Conversify is not a replacement for professional therapy. While our AI chatbot and resources can provide immediate support, coping strategies, and education about mental health, they are designed to complement professional care, not replace it. Our platform can help you between therapy sessions, assist during moments when immediate professional help isn't available, and connect you with licensed therapists when needed.

Conversify offers several pricing options:

  • Free Basic Access: Includes limited AI chatbot conversations, access to selected resources, and community forums.
  • Premium Plan ($9.99/month): Unlimited AI chatbot support, full access to all resources and ASMR tools, personalized recommendations, and ad-free experience.
  • Family Plan ($19.99/month): All Premium features for up to 5 family members.
  • Annual Subscriptions: Save 20% with annual billing on any paid plan.

We also offer subsidized access for those who cannot afford our services - please contact our support team for details.

Yes, we take your privacy and security extremely seriously. Conversify implements the following measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all conversations with our AI chatbot
  • HIPAA-compliant data storage and processing
  • Stringent data protection policies
  • Option to use the platform anonymously in Guest Mode
  • Regular security audits by independent third parties
  • We never sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties

You can review our complete Privacy Policy for more detailed information.

Getting started with Conversify is simple:

  1. Create a free account using your email, or sign in with Google or Facebook
  2. Complete a brief wellbeing assessment (optional but recommended for personalized support)
  3. Start chatting with our AI assistant by clicking the chat button on any page
  4. Explore our resource library and ASMR tools based on your interests and needs
  5. Join our community forums to connect with others on similar journeys

You can also use our platform in Guest Mode without creating an account, although some features will be limited.

Our AI chatbot uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning algorithms trained on evidence-based therapeutic approaches like Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Mindfulness, and Positive Psychology. It can understand your messages, identify emotional states, and provide appropriate responses, coping strategies, and resources. The chatbot learns from interactions to provide increasingly personalized support while maintaining strict privacy standards.

The Conversify AI is designed to detect crisis language and respond appropriately. When a potential crisis is identified, the system will:

  • Acknowledge the seriousness of the situation immediately
  • Provide emergency contact information for your country
  • Offer to connect you directly to a crisis helpline
  • Present immediate coping strategies for distress tolerance
  • If available, connect you with an on-call mental health professional

Important: If you're experiencing a medical emergency or are in immediate danger, please call emergency services (911/999/112) directly.

Free accounts can access the AI chatbot for up to 5 conversations per week, with each conversation limited to 10 minutes. Premium subscribers receive unlimited access to the chatbot 24/7, with no time or frequency limitations. The chatbot is available on all devices and can be accessed through our website or mobile app.

Our resource library includes a diverse collection of content, including:

  • Guided meditation and mindfulness sessions (audio and video)
  • Educational videos about mental health conditions and coping strategies
  • Interactive exercises and worksheets based on evidence-based therapies
  • Motivational and inspirational content
  • Sleep improvement resources
  • Stress management techniques
  • Emotional regulation tools
  • Self-assessment questionnaires

All resources are developed or curated by mental health professionals and are updated regularly with new content.

Conversify uses a sophisticated recommendation system that considers multiple factors: your initial assessment results (if completed), your interactions with the AI chatbot, your browsing and usage patterns on the platform, resources you've previously engaged with, and feedback you've provided. The system continuously learns from your interactions to refine its recommendations. You can also manually adjust your preferences in your profile settings to receive more targeted suggestions.

ASMR (Autonomous Sensory Meridian Response) refers to the tingling sensation that typically begins on the scalp and moves down the back of the neck and upper spine in response to specific auditory or visual stimuli. Many people find ASMR content deeply relaxing and it can help with:

  • Reducing anxiety and stress
  • Improving sleep quality and reducing insomnia
  • Creating a sense of calm and relaxation
  • Providing a mental break during stressful situations
  • Reducing symptoms of depression

Our ASMR therapy section offers a variety of sounds and experiences designed to promote relaxation and wellbeing.

Yes, Premium subscribers can download ASMR sounds for offline use through our mobile app. This feature allows you to access your favorite sounds even without an internet connection, making it perfect for travel, areas with poor connectivity, or to avoid using mobile data. To download sounds, look for the download icon next to any ASMR track in the app. You can manage your downloaded content in the "My Library" section.

To reset your password:

  1. Click on "Login" at the top right of the screen
  2. Select "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Click "Send Reset Link"
  5. Check your email (including spam/junk folders) for a password reset link
  6. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, please contact our support team for assistance.

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Log in to your Conversify account
  2. Go to "Account Settings" (click your profile icon at the top right)
  3. Select "Subscription" from the menu
  4. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  5. Follow the prompts to confirm cancellation

Please note: When you cancel, you'll continue to have access to Premium features until the end of your current billing period. We don't provide partial refunds for unused portions of your subscription period. If you have any issues with cancellation, please contact our support team.
